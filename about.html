Our Philosophy

Rooted in Precision and Care

Dr. Yasmine Soliman is a dedicated orthodontic specialist with more than twenty years of clinical experience focused exclusively on orthodontic care. Holding a BDS from Cairo University, a Master’s degree in Orthodontics, and being a member of the American Aligners, she has developed a refined approach centred on precision, communication, and long-term patient confidence.

Throughout her professional career, she has managed a wide range of orthodontic cases involving growth modification, fixed appliances, and clear aligner therapy, combining advanced clinical planning with an individualised understanding of every patient’s needs. Her philosophy is rooted in the belief that orthodontic treatment is not only about creating balanced smiles, but also about improving comfort, self-confidence, and overall quality of life.

Alongside her private practice, Dr. Yasmine continues her clinical contribution through Cairo University’s orthodontic services in Sheikh Zayed, supporting patients who may not otherwise have access to specialist orthodontic care. She believes that trust, clarity, and a positive treatment environment are essential parts of a successful orthodontic journey — one that often accompanies patients over several years.
